Step 0

Get access

Before you can use the bot, your Telegram ID needs to be registered.

1
Find your Telegram ID Open Telegram → search for @userinfobot → start it. It replies with your numeric ID.
2
Example ID format 387654321
3
Send it to your administrator Share your numeric ID with your manager or finance admin. Wait for confirmation.
4
Open the bot Once confirmed, visit: t.me/malakut_expense_report_bot
Step 1

Open a pack

A "pack" = one expense report for one trip or period.

What is a pack? Each pack groups all receipts for a single trip or period. One pack open at a time.

Type in the Telegram chat:

You: open pack Zurich May 2026
Bot: ✅ Pack "Zurich May 2026" opened
📌
Naming tip City + month, project name, or any label that identifies the period.
🔒
One active pack at a time Stays open until you run /compile.
Step 2

Add receipts

Send documents in the chat. The bot reads them automatically.

📷
Single receipt
Send as a Photo
📄
Multi-page doc
Send as a PDF file
🍎 iOS
Files → select pages → Share → Create PDF
🤖 Android
Google Drive → + → Scan → Save as PDF
Bot: Found: Marriott — 520.00 AED. Add this?
Reply yes to confirm, no to cancel
You: yes
Bot: ✅ Receipt added to pack
Auto-detection

Expense categories

The bot reads each document and assigns a category automatically.

🚕
Transportation
Taxi, Uber, Careem, airline, train, metro, parking
🏨
Hotel
Hotel, Airbnb, any accommodation
🍽
Business meetings
Restaurant, café, conference, client lunch
📦
Other
Everything that doesn't fit the above categories
You can always override a category later — see Step 4 (Fix mistakes).

Step 4

Fix mistakes

First run /showpack to see item numbers, then use these commands.

change type for #3 to Hotel Reclassify an item's expense category
fix vendor for #1 — Marriott Correct a misread vendor name
fix amount for #2 — 150 Update a wrong amount
fix date for #1 — 2026-05-10 Correct a misread date
delete #2 Remove an item from the pack
⚠️ The bot always asks for confirmation before applying any change. You must reply yes to proceed.
Step 5

Generate the report

When all receipts are confirmed, produce your PDF expense report.

👁
/preview
Get a PDF preview without closing the pack. Review before finalizing.
✅
/compile
Generate the final PDF and close the pack. Cannot be undone.
📤
The bot sends the PDF directly in chat The report is also automatically forwarded to accounting — nothing to do on your end.
Reference

Tips & reminders

Keep these in mind for smooth expense reporting.

✅
Always confirm or cancel the bot's draft before sending the next receipt.
📄
Send multi-page documents as PDF, not photos — better accuracy, cleaner parsing.
📅
All dates are shown in YYYY-MM-DD format.
💬
Stuck? Type help in the chat anytime for a command list.
📞
Need access or ran into an issue?
Contact your administrator — they can register your Telegram ID and troubleshoot problems.
